WHAT WE DO

Do you need roofing services in the Auckland region? Markus & The Brothers Ltd is Auckland based roofing company. Experts in roof restorations, repairs & Maintenance. Check out our services below.

Roof Restoration & Repairs

Restoration is an upgrade and rejuvenation of an existing roof and it can be undertaken on various roof types. The most commonly restored roofs are concrete, metal, decramastic & long run metal roofing. There are 3 stages involved in the process of restoration: chemical & waterflushing stage, repair stage and roof painting stage.
